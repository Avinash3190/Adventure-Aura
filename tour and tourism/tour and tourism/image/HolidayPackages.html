Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay for my tour package?
We accept almost all modes of payment, such as cash, net banking, Paytm, Gpay, and bank transfer.

Is my booking refundable?
The refundability of your booking depends on your tour package. For clear details, please reach out to our customer support.

Does requesting a quote cost me?
Absolutely not. We believe in fostering relationships, which is why we offer free consultations and provide the best possible quotes.

How can I cancel my trip?
We’re sorry to hear you’re canceling your travel plans with us. But rest assured, we have a cancellation policy in place. To find out if you qualify for a refund upon cancellation, please contact our customer support.

Can you accommodate a specific budget? 
At International Youth Club, we strive to offer top-notch services at affordable prices. While we typically suggest selecting from our pre-built tour packages, we’re also willing to tailor a travel itinerary to fit your budget.
